RateOple global shell refactor results

Mobile drawer/account follow-up:
- Fixed the mobile hamburger and avatar panels so they render as usable full-height viewport drawers instead of being clipped to the sticky header area.
- Root cause: the mobile overlays were rendered inside `.site-header`, whose sticky blurred surface and overflow/stacking context clipped the fixed-position drawer content. That left only the drawer header/top area visible.
- Exact fix: moved mobile menu, account, and search panels into a `createPortal(..., document.body)` viewport layer; raised `.mobile-overlay` z-index; added `100dvh` drawer sizing; kept drawer body as a flex child with vertical scrolling and hidden horizontal overflow.
- Preserved panel behavior: opening menu/search/account closes the other panels, overlay click closes, Escape closes, close buttons close, and link taps navigate and close the active panel.
- Aligned the media label to "TV Series" in English so the mobile drawer matches the required navigation wording.
